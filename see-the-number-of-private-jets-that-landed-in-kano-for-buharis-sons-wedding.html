See The Number Of Private Jets That landed In Kano For Buhari's Son's Wedding

 Posted on May 2, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  144 words  |  Chauncey Koziol

A video of numerous private jets that landed at the private tarmac  of the Aminu Kano International Airport for President Buhari’s son’s wedding has surfaced on social media

Naija News reports that President Muhammadu Buhari’s son, Yusuf Buhari, today, married his lover the daughter of the Emir of Bichi, Alhaji Nasir Ado Bayero, Zahra.

The wedding pulled strong socialites, business icons, top government functionaries and politicians to Kano in celebration and support for the president and his family.

In the video shared by an eyewitness, the private jets lined up at the international wing of Aminu Kano International Airport, numbered over 30.
