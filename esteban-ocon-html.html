Esteban Ocon - Age, Family, Bio

 Posted on April 2, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  94 words  |  Lourie Helzer

Race car driver who made his Formula One debut for Manor Racing in the 2016 Belgian Grand Prix. He also came in first in the GP3 Series with the ART Grand Prix in 2015. He has over 2.2 million followers on his estebanocon Instagram account.  

Before Fame

He won the French Cadet Championship when he started karting at age 10. 

Trivia

He joined Sahara Force India in 2017.

Family Life

His parents' names are Sabrina and Laurent Ocon. He dated Elena Berri from 2018 until 2022. 

Associated With

He replaced Rio Haryanto in his first Formula One race.
