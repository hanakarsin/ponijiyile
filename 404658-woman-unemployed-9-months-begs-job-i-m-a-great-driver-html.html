A Nigerian lady, Mary Allen, has taken her job-hunting frustration to LinkedIn where she narrated how she has been without employment for nine months.

Savings running out

Mary, who lives in Port Harcourt, said all the money she had saved up was fast running out as there were no sources of income.

The lady disclosed that at a point in those months, she told herself that if nothing turned up, she would go into driving for a taxi-hailing service.

Mary added that she would excel in that since she was a good driver with an amazing customer service experience.

The lady, therefore, called for help, asking people to connect her with any hire purchase dealership that could give a car on an instalment payment plan.

The determined Nigerian even said she did not mind relocating if she gets a car in another state. She ended her post with:

“After all, the only way after hitting rock bottom is going upward.”
