Homeless man's reaction to receiving help leaves internet swooning

 Posted on June 1, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  460 words  |  Chauncey Koziol

- A generous young man recently shared a clip of a homeless man's reaction to receiving some helpful gifts

- In the TikTok video, the generous man offers to help make the homeless man's life easier by asking him what he needs

- He then proceeded to buy all the things the homeless man needed to make life a lot easier on the streets

A heartwarming clip has been shared online by a man with the Instagram handle @isaiahgarzaintl, who is seen helping a homeless man.

In the TikTok video, the kind man asks the homeless man what would put a smile on his face.

To which the homeless guy responded that a tent, a blanket and some food would really make him happy.

Not long into the clip, the man leaves to buy all the things and gives them to the pleasantly surprised homeless guy.

"I got you a blanket, a tent. I know you needed a tent, right? Food brother. I'm also gonna give you 200 dollars (KSh 20,000)," the Good Samaritan said as he handed the goodies over to the young man."This is the best day of my life," replied the homeless man with a smile on his face.

Many of the generous man's Instagram followers loved the heartwarming post. A lot of them flocked to the comment section to commend him for his kind-heart.
