Children are a bundle of joy and blessings in our lives. Therefore, childbearing and naming are a big deal. The inner desire to give your child the best name may end up in frustrations for failing to get the right meanings. Notice that the significance of a name is far much powerful than how good it sounds. For this reason, South African baby names and their meanings are useful for anyone expecting a little one.

The process of choosing between different child names is overwhelming and often ends up with undecided or disagreeing parents. The choice can be effortless with a full list of popular South African names at hand. The fact that a name influences a child’s future is yet to lose its meaning and, therefore, an essential factor to consider.

South African baby names

There are hundreds of South African baby names from where you can find a suitable identity for your kid. Luckily, these names reflect the diversity of South African culture and way of life.

What is the most common name in South Africa?

While there are hundreds of lovely baby names in SA, most people prefer naming their children Enzokuhle (Do good). The name emerged in the top place following recent statistics mostly because it is suitable for both girls and boys. Dlamini also qualified as the most popular surname. Closely following were names such as Ndlovu and Nkosi.
