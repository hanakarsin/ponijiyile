'You Lied To Me' - Uti Nwachukwu Reacts As Peggy Ovire, Frederick Leonard Tie Knot

 Posted on April 24, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  326 words  |  Almeda Bohannan

Media personality, Uti Nwachukwu, has jokingly teased Nollywood actress, Peggy Ovire, for lying about her romantic relationship with her colleague, Frederick Leonard.

Naija News reports that Peggy and Frederick would be getting married today, in a star-studded event in Delta State.

Peggy took to her Instagram page, a few hours before the event to share their loved-up photo, stating that their union is something of a miracle.

She prayed that their marriage will last forever and they would always put God first in everything.

She wrote: “This Love we have is Something Of a Miracle. It’s so easy to know that you’re the other half that I’m destined to Spend the rest of my life with, Diamonds are Forever and So is Our LOVE!

“May we always put GOD First in Our Marriage & May Our Lives be Sweeter than honey. Let’s do this Baby. I Love You”

Gushing over the couple, Uti Nwachukwu said Peggy lied to him as he recalled an interview where the Nollywood actress claimed she and Frederick were just friends.

Some social media users also confirmed to watching the interview where Peggy debunked dating her colleague, Frederick.

Peggy Ovire Slams Fan

Meanwhile, Peggy Ovire has slammed a fan who expressed fear about her marriage ending up like that of Yul Edochie and Judy Austin.

Peggy took to Instagram to share a romantic picture of Frederick and another lady.

The actress shared the picture to publicize the new movie of Leonard which premiered on Youtube.

In the picture, Frederick could be seen all loved up with his colleague, Mary Igwe in the movie titled ‘Stay’.

However, a fan of Peggy who seemed not comfortable with the movie poster warned the actress, saying she hopes that Mary Igwe and Leonard do not end up like Yul Edochie and Judy Austin.

Displeased with the statement, Peggy warned the fan to avoid such comments on her page, or else she will get blocked.
