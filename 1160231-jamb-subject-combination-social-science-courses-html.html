What are the Social Science subjects in Nigeria?

The Social Science subjects accepted by Nigerian tertiary institutions are Mathematics, English, Commerce, Geography, Economics, Accounting, Marketing, Agriculture, Government and Christian Religious knowledge. Examples of cources where these subjects are applicable include:

  • JAMB subject combination for Sociology: English and three subjects from the fields of Social Science or Arts.
  • JAMB subject combination for Psychology: English and any three subjects chosen from Arts or Social Science disciplines.
  • JAMB subject combination for Mass Communication: English and any three subjects from Arts and Social Science fields.
  • JAMB subject combination for Political Science: English, Government or History, and two other subjects from Social Science or Arts.
  • JAMB subject combination for Business Administration: English, Mathematics, Economics, and any other Social Science subject.
  • JAMB subject combination for Economics: English, Mathematics, Economics, and one additional subject from Government, History, Geography, Literature in English, or French.

What are the compulsory subjects in JAMB?

There is only one compulsory subject for all JAMB exams, and that is English Language. Other required subjects will depend on the course of study you are applying for.

How many courses are there in Social Science?

There are more than 60 courses one can pursue. Some include Business Administration, Mass Communication, Sociology and Anthropology, Public Administration, and Human Resources Management.

What are the subjects for Social Science in JAMB?

The major Social Science subjects combination include literature in English, Government, Economics, Geography, History, commerce, and the Principle of Accounting.

How many years does it take to study Social Science?

Most institutions require four years to complete a Bachelor's degree in Social Science. Depending on the individual, this could include full-time or part-time studies.

Is Criminology a Social Science?

Yes, Criminology is a social science course that draws on various social science disciplines, including demography, psychology, and geography. Students majoring in criminology learn about the biological, psychological, and social causes of crime, such as socioeconomic status.

Is biology a Social Science subject?

Biology is a natural science in which researchers investigate living organisms and what makes them up. Genetics, environmental biology, plant biology, zoology, biochemistry, and molecular biology will be covered, with opportunities to specialise as your interests develop.

What are the JAMB requirement?

The general JAMB registration requirements include having the following:

  • National Identification Number (NIN)
  • O'level results (WAEC, NECO, or NABTEB)
  • JAMB registration fee
  • JAMB profile code
  • Valid email address and phone number
  • Passport ohotograph

What is a good JAMB score?

JAMB has a general minimum score of 180 to be eligible for admission into any Nigerian university. Individual universities and specific courses may have higher cut-off points.
