Pingel said he was told on Sunday evening by Roberts' partner, Lance O'Brien, that the beauty had passed away.

O'Brien believed the actress was not going to pull through after she was hospitalised.

On Monday morning, O'Brien was told by doctors that his partner was still alive.

Roberts is well known for her role in the 1985 James Bond film, A View To A Kill, in which she played Stacey Sutton.

She also starred in Charlie's Angels as Julie Rogers in its fifth season and had other roles in films such as Sheena, The Beastmaster and Tourist Trap.
