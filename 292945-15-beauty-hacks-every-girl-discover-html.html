15 beauty hacks for makeup that every girl should know

 Posted on May 28, 2024  |  7 minutes  |  1425 words  |  Chauncey Koziol

Do you want to know how to apply makeup like a pro but don't have years of experience or lack necessities such as makeup brushes or masks? If the answer is yes, then equipping yourself with the right beauty hacks for makeup is the way to go.

You don't have to spend hours in front of a mirror to get your makeup perfect. There are all kinds of little tricks you can use that will make applying makeup much easier and ensure that you always have a look you are going for.

Beauty hacks for makeup

Here are some incredible beauty hacks that every beauty queen should know:

1. Melting eye pencil liner to make it move smoothly

Any creamy makeup product tends to blend in better after being warmed up. Therefore, if your pencil eyeliner tends to skip or tug on your eyelid, or it requires you to apply more than one coat, this hack is perfect for you. Slightly melt down your pencil before you begin lining it.

To melt your eyeliner pencil, hold its tip under a lighter's flame for a few seconds or until it starts getting tacky. Let it cool before you swatch it on your arm to ensure it's not hot or melted. You will see the consistency change, and it's fantastic.

2. Using white eyeliner to make your eye shadow pop

If you want to make a less pigmented or sheer eye shadow look, this is one of the best eye makeup hacks to try out. To do this, thoroughly apply a white pencil eyeliner over your eyelid. Gently use your eye shadow on top of this coating. The opaque coverage used as the base with white eyeliner intensifies any eye shadow color regardless of how less pigmented it is, thus making it pop.

3. Outlining your cat-eye before filling it in

Do you struggle to nail perfectly winged eyeliner? Try outlining before filling it in. First, create the bottom section of your cat eye by extending a line beyond your lower lash line. Decide how thick you want your wing to be before outlining the upper part. Trace the top part for your cat-eye from your flick's endpoint to the upper lash line.

4. Hanging a mason jar on the wall

Mason jars will never go out of fashion when used as makeup organizing hacks. For this DIY project, use a sticker to hang the pot on the wall.

5. Using a spoon like a stencil winger eyeliner

Sometimes without adequate practice, it isn't straightforward to use your free hand to draw your cat-eye. If this is the case, you need this fantastic beauty hack for makeup. Pick a spoon and hold its stem against your eye's outer corners.

The first step is to grab your liquid eyeliner and draw a straight line for your cat-eye. The second step is to flip your spoon and place it in a position as though it is hugging your eyelid, and with the use of the rounded outer edge, craft a perfectly curved winged effect on your eye.

6. Creating a voluminous mascara

One of the perfect makeup hacks for glasses is creating volume on your mascara rather than lengthening them. For ladies wearing glasses, you should curl your eyelashes before applying mascara that adds volume instead of making them longer.

7. Creating a smoky eye using a hashtag

A smoky eye is one of the best makeup designs ladies go for whenever they want to create an outstanding masterpiece. However, if you are not sure what you are doing while creating this design, the result can go from sultry to sloppy in a flash of a second. Luckily, with this makeup hack, you can be assured to attain the best.

With the use of creamy eyeliner, create a slanted hashtag symbol on the outer edge of your eyelid. Using a smudging brush or a beauty blender, blend out the hashtag symbol and repeat the same on the other eye. It will give you a result that is not only symmetrical but also prevents you from getting a little lazy with doing your eyeliner.

8. Shielding your mascara with a spoon

Have you ever done your eye shadow so correctly, but it got some mascara smudges ruins? There is nothing as annoying as that. However, with this eye makeup hack, you no longer have to go through the trouble. Hold the spoon in a position as though it's hugging your eyelid as you apply the mascara.

With the spoon in this position, you will be sweeping your mascara wand against your eyelashes and the spoon, thus making the residues stick on the back of the spoon instead of your perfectly done eye shadow.

9. Matching your liner with your frames

Sometimes, it is hard to know how to match the perfect coating for someone wearing glasses. You should opt to wear a thick eyeliner if your frames are thick and a thin eyeliner if you have thin frames for your glasses.9

10. Reviving mascara

Nothing is as annoying as dried up mascara, especially when you are looking forward to going for full-face makeup. However, there are amazing and cheap makeup hacks that will come in handy for you when this happens to your mascara.

To revive dried mascara, pour three to four drops of saline solution into your mascara bottle, and this will instantly restore its initial smooth consistency. However, it is advisable not to use mascara after three months because past this, there is a likelihood of the mascara to collect bacteria that would lead to serious eye infections.

11. Using powder to plump your eyelashes

If you want to make your lashes appear voluminous and fuller, this is one of the best makeup tips and hacks for you. Once you have correctly done your mascara, grab your translucent setting powder and dust some of it on the lashes in between the mascara coats. The powder will create a fuller effect by helping grip the mascara in between each layer.

12. Blotting excess oil with a clean toilet seat cover

Although this might sound gross, it is a perfect makeup for oil skin hack that works wonders. Toilet seat covers and blotting paper are manufactured with the same material. Therefore, the two can be used in place of the other, which means that you can soak the excess oil on your skin with the toilet seat cover. Pick a clean seat cover and gently pat it over your T-zones to reduce the excess oil on your face.

13. Using mug or glasses as makeup containers

Sometimes, you don't have to buy expensive storage containers to store your makeup. Instead, grab that amazing mug that you no longer use and use it to store some of your beauty products. It is not only one of the cheapest makeup organizing hacks but also a classy and unique one.

14. Heating eyelash curler with a hairdryer

Heat is known to cause a faster curling effect on hair. Similarly, adding some warmth to your eyelash curler with help in curling them faster while still maintaining the curls for long. For you to achieve this DIY beauty hack, heat the curler using your blow dryer and leave it for some seconds to cool off. Use the inside part of your arm to test the heat to avoid burning your eyelid. Lastly, clamp down the eyelashes and curl them gently.

15. Mixing petroleum jelly with eyeshadow color

Take your desired eye shadow color loose pigments and mix it with a little bit of your petroleum powder, such as Vaseline. You can combine this on a spoon or in a small petroleum jelly container. Apply the mixture on your lips and boom; you have your custom-made lip gloss. This is one of the easiest DIY beauty hacks.
