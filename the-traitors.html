The Traitors

 Posted on May 18, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  413 words  |  Hettie Henneman

The cast of Peacock's reality TV sensation, The Traitors, features a thrilling mix of reality stars and celebrities competing in a high-stakes murder mystery game. Among them are cunning traitors and determined faithfuls. The traitors aim to eliminate the faithfuls one by one, while the faithfuls strive to unmask and banish the traitors from the game.

If the faithful contestants successfully make it to the end, they'll win the prize and share it among themselves. But if a traitor prevails, they'll walk away with all the money, leaving the faithfuls empty-handed.

How much does the winner of The Traitors get?

Contestants on The Traitors can win up to $250,000 based on their mission success. The prize fund increases with their achievements.

Who hosts The Traitors?

The Traitors is hosted by the award-winning actor Alan Cumming. You might recognize Alan from his iconic roles in movies such as GoldenEye (1995), Josie and the Pussycats (2001), My Old School (2022), X2: X-Men United (2003), and his unforgettable portrayal of the nerdy-turned-wealthy Sandy Frink in Romy and Michele’s High School Reunion (1997).

Where is The Traitors filmed?

The Traitors is filmed at Ardross Castle, a 19th-century estate located in the Scottish Highlands, approximately 30 miles from Inverness. Ironically, Scotland is also Alan’s home country.

Who has won The Traitors?

Season 1: Cirie Fields won Season 1 as a traitor after the competitors secured the full $250,000 prize fund.

Season 2: CT Tamburello and Trishelle Cannatella won Season 2, splitting the jackpot of $208,100.

Where to watch The Traitors?

You can stream new episodes and past seasons of The Traitors on Peacock with a paid subscription. Season 2 aired Thursdays at 9 p.m. EST, but we’ll have to wait for Season 3, expected in early 2025, to see the new schedule.
