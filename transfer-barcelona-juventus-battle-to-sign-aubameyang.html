Barcelona, Juventus Battle To Sign Aubameyang

 Posted on February 6, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  151 words  |  Almeda Bohannan

La Liga side, Barcelona and Serie A club, Juventus have indicated interest in signining Arsenal former captain, Pierre-Emerick Aubameyang.

The Gabonese has not played for the Gunners since the club manager Mikel Arteta stripped him of the captaincy following disciplinary breaches.

The North London side are now trying to offload their striker, who was signed in a £56m deal from Dortmund four years ago.

The Gunners are discussing with Barcelona and Juventus as the two clubs are interested in signing the striker on loan.

Saudi Arabian club, Al Hilal, has also reached out to Arsenal to sign Aubameyang and they are ready to cover his £350,000-a-week salary in full, which Barcelona and Juventus might find difficult.

It is believed that Aubameyang wants to remain in Europe so as to play at the highest level.

The Gabon striker has been left out of the travelling squad to Dubai for warm-weather training.
