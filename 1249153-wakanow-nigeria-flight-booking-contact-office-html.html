Background of Wakanow Company

The company was established in 2008. It is the pioneer online travel agent in Nigeria that makes travelling hassle-free. Since its establishment, the company has grown and now has physical offices in Nigeria, Kenya, Ghana, the United Kingdom and the United States of America. The staff members are trained to offer excellent customer services, the modes of payment are convenient, and the use of updated technologies enhances customer service.

The company’s vision is:

“To be our customers’indispensable and trusted travel companion. To constantly excite them with travel deals, packages and services that are unobtainable elsewhere.”

The mission is:

“To make planning, payment and travel simple and enjoyable by building a world-class, secure travel system manned by experienced, Customer-friendly, travel experts.”

The core principles that guide its operations are transparency, integrity and honesty.

    Will the company get me a visa for my international travel?

    The company has a program that assists you in acquiring your travel visa within a short period. Many Nigerians are unable to travel because they are unable to procure visas to their dream destinations. The company helps their customers with this process. However, the issuance of visas is the role of embassies and not the company. The company, therefore, assists you with the process of acquiring the visa but does not guarantee that you will get it.
