A courageous Kenyan man from Eldoret has ignited mixed reactions online after he revealed that he was looking for a second wife, but many think his demands are ambiguous.

The 30-year-old man said he was financially stable and his second wife would benefit a lot if they match. He said the woman or rather women who'll contact him, should be ready to prove that she is the one during the first three months.

"I'm 30 years financially stable working as a manager somewhere. I'm looking for a second wife should have no children. The first 3 months is an internship to check her performance and behaviour, upon confirmation she will receive benefits i.e a new house in Eldoret, medical cover and others," he wrote to Facebook bigwig AKL.

Netizens reacted to man's demands, with some claiming he was after using women and dumping them, advising applicants to think twice.
